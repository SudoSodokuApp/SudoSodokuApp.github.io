Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

SudoSodoku is built on a simple rule: your data is yours. The app collects nothing, tracks nothing, and phones home to no one.

What stays on your device

Everything. Your puzzles, solve history, statistics, ELO rating, and settings are stored in a single local file on your iPhone. This data is never transmitted to us or to any third party. We operate no servers and have no access to it.

Game Center (optional)

If you choose to sign in to Apple's Game Center, your leaderboard scores (rating and solve times) and achievement unlocks are submitted to Game Center so they can appear on leaderboards. This is handled entirely by Apple under the Apple Privacy Policy. The app is fully playable offline as a guest without Game Center.

What we don't do

  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No accounts, no email collection
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Deleting your data

Delete the app, and every trace of your data on the device goes with it. Game Center data, if any, can be managed through your Apple Account settings.
